About us


Sir Geoffrey Leigh Academy is a centre of educational excellence serving the whole community of Dartford. Our students meet and work together in a well-ordered and harmonious community.

We are a high-performing school which takes students of all abilities. Our success is built on the traditional values of pride, respect and hard work in a modern, hi-tech learning environment. Our performance is regularly rated among the top ten state schools nationally for value added.

Opening in 1990 as one of only fifteen City Technology Colleges across the country, SGLA has been at the forefront of education. We are constantly looking for new ways to make learning exciting and powerful. As a City Academy from September 2007, we now work more closely with the Local Authority and other local schools, while retaining our independence and our own unique culture.


Project: The students should learn the 3Rs


Respect

Our students will be learning to give respect. Respect is a discipline, which all of our students will have before leaving our school. We believe that Respect is something that we should always give to the people around us.

Responsibility

Our students will be learning to be Responsible for their actions. Responsibility is also a discipline, which our students will have before leaving our school. We believe that everyone should be Responsible for their actions.

Resilience

Our students will be also learning to have resilience. Resilience is a ability to overcome all the challenges they will face in their lives and our students will have resilience before they leave our school. We believe that not having resilience is one of the biggest reason why people are attempting sucide, so if our students have the resilience then that will not be a problem for our students in the future.
